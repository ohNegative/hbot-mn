About Hyperbaric Centers of Minnesota
A patient centered approach to healing.

Patient Centered Care

Minnesota Hyperbaric Treatment Center is an outpatient clinic that specializes in providing safe and affordable Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) for a variety of conditions. Our oxygen chambers are comfortable and many patients find sessions incredibly relaxing. Treatments are supervised by an attending hyperbaric therapist at all times.

Our Technology

Minnesota Hyperbaric Treatment Center has three chambers in which the therapy is administered. A chamber is a clear, acrylic vessel that a patient lies in comfortably during his or her treatment. Typically, a patient will receive the hyperbaric oxygen therapy for approximately 90 minutes each day, five days a week for an average period of six weeks. This period can be shorter or longer based on the reason for treatment.

Our Partners in Healing

At Minnesota Hyperbaric Treatment we also strive to establish mutually beneficial business relationships with all of our providers. Our commitment to health and healing starts with our providers and staff. We will work with you and your provider to find a suitable plan for your individual needs.
